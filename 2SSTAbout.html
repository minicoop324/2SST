Every Brilliant Thing

Anna Biggerstaff is a junior double major in Directing and Acting at Lipscomb University. Theatre has been Anna's most brilliant thing since middle school. She has notably been an ASM for Nashville Rep's Inherit the Wind, Assistant Director and the Officer in Lipscomb University's Tartuffe, and the president of Lipscomb University's improv team: Self-Improv-ment. Anna wants to thank this incredible team of women who has believed in her every step of this process, her friends that are her biggest cheerleaders, and her family for their support. She hopes that this show will help you love the many brilliant things in life. Enjoy the show!

Kate is thrilled to be working with Second Stage Student Theatre for the first time. She has had a blast and can’t wait to continue her involvement with this company. She couldn’t have done it, however, without the help of Abby Wyatt. To quote Brooke Bethel: she is a “beautiful and brave spinach leaf.” She’s thankful to everyone who has supported her and to everyone who has donated their time or personal items to this show. She hopes everyone enjoys the show!

Katherine Broome is so thrilled to be a part of the Every Brilliant Thing team! She is always excited to work with 2SST; you may have seen her in last year’s production of “Hedda Gabler.” Katherine is a Sophomore Theatre Education major at Lipscomb. She would like to thank her family, her roommates, and to the 2SST producers for giving her the opportunity to be a part of this show! Enjoy!
